## Tone: Punchy

Maximum signal. Minimum words.

---

## Headline rules

- Extremely short
- High impact
- No filler

Examples:
- "Fix the fragmentation"
- "One platform. Full visibility."

---

## Body copy rules

- Write in fragments if needed
- Remove all non-essential words
- Prioritize speed and clarity

---

## Bullet rules

Each bullet:
- 5–8 words max
- one clear idea

Examples:
- "Unify data across all channels"
- "Automate key decision flows"

Avoid:
- long sentences
- explanatory language

---

## Language rules

Use:
- strong verbs
- simple words

Avoid:
- corporate phrasing
- passive voice

---

## Tone behavior by slide type

Solution:
- sharp, modular ideas

Value:
- bold, direct outcomes

Proof:
- only key stats

---

## Sentence style

- very short
- high energy
- direct

---

## Examples

Bad:
"This solution enables improved customer engagement"

Good:
"Drive real-time engagement"
