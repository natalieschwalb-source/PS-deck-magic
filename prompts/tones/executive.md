## Tone: Executive

Clarity. Precision. Decisions.

---

## Headline rules

- One idea per headline
- Must be decision-oriented
- No filler words

Structure:
[action or outcome] + [business impact]

Examples:
- "Reduce cost-to-serve by 30%"
- "Enable real-time customer insight across channels"

---

## Body copy rules

- Get to the point immediately
- No context-setting fluff
- Every sentence must add value

Structure:
[statement] → [impact]

---

## Bullet rules

Each bullet:
- one idea only
- short and direct
- business-relevant

Avoid:
- multi-clause bullets
- storytelling

---

## Language rules

Use:
- enable
- reduce
- increase
- deliver
- accelerate

Avoid:
- adjectives without evidence
- emotional language
- vague phrasing

---

## Tone behavior by slide type

Solution:
- focus on what is being delivered

Value:
- clearly state business outcomes

Proof:
- highlight results, not narrative

---

## Sentence style

- short sentences
- direct language
- no unnecessary words

---

## Examples

Bad:
"This initiative will significantly improve performance"

Good:
"Reduce processing time by 40% within 12 months"
