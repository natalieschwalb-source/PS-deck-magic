## Tone: Data-Driven

Every claim earns its place. Numbers lead. Evidence closes.

---

## Headline rules

- Must include a number, benchmark, or measurable target
- Structure:
  [metric] + [context] + [baseline/timeframe]

---

## Body copy rules

- First sentence contains a measurable claim
- Structure:
  [claim] → [basis] → [implication]

---

## Bullet rules

Each bullet:
- metric + change + implication

---

## Stat rules

- Always include real or directional numbers
- Always include context

---

## Language rules

BANNED:
- significantly
- dramatically
- greatly

Replace with:
- numbers
- ranges
- benchmarks

---

## Tone behavior by slide type

Solution:
- quantify impact

Proof:
- anchor in evidence

Value:
- express measurable outcomes

---

## Sentence style

- short
- dense
- precise

---

## Examples

"Typically delivers 30–50% reduction in manual effort within 12–18 months"
