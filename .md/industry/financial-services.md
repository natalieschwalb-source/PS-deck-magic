# Financial Services

## Positioning
Publicis Sapient helps financial institutions break down legacy silos, modernize platforms, and shift from product-centric to customer-centric operating models.

## Market Segments
- Payments: processors, issuers, networks
- Banking: retail, commercial, investment
- Insurance: general, commercial, health
- Capital Markets: brokers, exchanges, custodians
- Investment Management: asset managers, wealth, hedge funds
- Government & Regulators

## Core Problems We Solve
- Organizational silos blocking transformation
- Legacy systems slowing innovation
- Poor customer experience across channels
- Inability to leverage data for personalization
- Regulatory pressure with outdated tech stacks

## Solution Themes
- Platform modernization (core banking, insurance systems)
- Data & identity-driven personalization
- Customer experience transformation
- Digital product innovation
- Internal capability enablement (not just delivery)

## Proof Points
- 12% of Publicis Groupe revenue
- 5,800+ specialists
- Leader in Asset & Wealth Management Services (HFS)

## Reusable Claims (for decks)
- "From siloed institutions to connected financial ecosystems"
- "Modernizing the core while unlocking customer value"
- "Turning data into competitive advantage"

## Executive Hooks
- "Break silos. Unlock growth."
- "Modern core. Smarter decisions."
- "From products to relationships."

## Where to Push / Where to Be Careful
Push:
- Transformation enablement (not just delivery)
- Data + CX + platform combined

- Overpromising speed in regulated environments
- Generic “digital transformation” language
