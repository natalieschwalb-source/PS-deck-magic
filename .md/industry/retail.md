# Retail

## Positioning
Publicis Sapient helps retailers become truly omnichannel, connecting supply chain, commerce, and customer experience into a unified, data-driven ecosystem.

## Market Segments
- Grocery
- Specialty retail
- Big box
- Department stores
- B2B retail (industrial, distribution)

## Core Problems We Solve
- Fragmented online and offline experiences
- Supply chain inefficiencies
- Low e-commerce profitability
- Poor inventory visibility
- Lack of personalization at scale

## Solution Themes
- Omnichannel commerce platforms
- Supply chain modernization
- Data-driven retail operations
- Personalization & loyalty systems
- Store experience transformation

## Proof Points
- 9% of Publicis Groupe revenue
- 2,500+ specialists
- Leader in Intelligent Retail Ecosystems (2025)

## Reusable Claims (for decks)
- "From channels to connected commerce"
- "Turning supply chains into growth engines"
- "Making every interaction shoppable"

## Executive Hooks
- "One customer. One experience."
- "From inventory to insight."
- "Commerce without friction."

## Where to Push / Where to Be Careful
Push:
- Omnichannel + supply chain connection (key differentiator)
- Profitability (not just experience)

Avoid:
- Over-indexing on UX without operational backbone
